Kochava Plugin Setup

Visit our Unity Support Documentation for more detailed documentation and non-typical integrations.

Step 1

Sign up for a free or paid account at:

https://kochava.com/unity

Step 2

Once you've created your account, ensure you're logged in and click the Create an App link within the welcome email you received. Follow the instructions to create an app GUID for each app you'd like to integrate.

Alternatively, if you would like to enable autoprovisioning of all new apps that are integrated, please contact your Kochava Client Success Manager.

Step 3

Within Unity, after importing the Kochava package, drag and drop the Kochava Configuration prefab object into the scene in which you'd like to initialize the Kochava SDK, which should occur as early as possible. Next, within the prefab inspector window under App GUID Configuration, enter the app GUID(s) you created into each of the applicable platform fields for this app. All other options within the prefab inspector need no adjustment or can be left blank.

NOTE: Unity Analytics must also be enabled and active in order to utilize the Kochava Plugin.

Alternatively, if you've setup autoprovisioning, leave the App GUID fields blank and enter the partner name provided by your Client Success Manager into the Kochava Partner Name field and enter a unique, permanent app name into the Kochava Partner App field.

Platform Specific Steps

When targeting Android and Windows UWP platforms, additional steps may be required if not already done so. These additional steps, if applicable, are described below.

When building for Android and publishing to the Google Play Store, Google Play Services must be present in order to collect the Android Advertising ID. At a minimum, Google Play Services “play-services-ads-identifier” and its dependencies be present within the app. For older versions or more information, see our detailed Support Documentation.

When building for Windows UWP, Unity version 2017+ is required and these Player Settings must be set:

  • API Compatibility Level: .NET 4.6
  • Permissions: Internet

That's it, you're done! At this point the Kochava SDK has been integrated and will begin attributing and reporting installation and session data. This analytics data will begin to appear within your Kochava dashboard as soon as the app is run.
